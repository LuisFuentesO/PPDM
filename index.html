About us

We are part of a new generation of not only video game players, but also computer science engineers who are willing to create, recreate and explore different video games from different genres, in order to improve our skills so we can make better, smarter and funnier video games.

In this page you will find out our different ideas and projects, and if you are interested in what you're reading, you can also contribute by sharing the ideas with your friends, or directly making a small donation to our cause. Adventure Chef

Design to be used with the Nintendo Wii U and 3DS touchscreen, Adventure Chef is a platforming game where you take the role of a chef and go to the unknown in the search of ingredients to create different kind of dishes... and food based weapons and tools! fighting dangerous food-monsters in your way, you will discover more than food in this crazy land.

Average Chilean Simulator

Acording to the Average Chilean, stuff like earthquakes is a day to day thing to happen, and with this game you will learn how to survive to earthquakes like the average chilean. By shaking your phone you need to readjust yourself so you don't get crushed in the next earthquake!
